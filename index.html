My Pricing

"How much do you charge?" is by far the most common first question I am asked. This is an understandable first question, but doesn't tackle the most important questions to ask of your web developer. Let's put things into focus.

A Tale of Two Pricing Systems

Fixed Bid

Everyone wants to know how much a website will cost. But a fixed bid estimate does a poor job of representing the natural variation of a task and the time required for it.

Time and Materials

Instead, I generally do a time & materials estimate - a fixed hourly rate applied to a good faith estimate of the range of time required to complete each component of the website. That way, the overall cost of the project is ultimately within your control. First estimate too expensive? No problem. We can trim the scope of the project to fit within your budget.
