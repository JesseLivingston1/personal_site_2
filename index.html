My Pricing

Cost is often the defining question for many clients. This is an understandable concern, but does not to focus on the essential qualities of a web developer.

Two Pricing Systems

Fixed Bid

Everyone wants to know how much a website will cost. But a fixed bid estimate can do a poor job of representing the natural variation of a task and the time required for it.

Time and Materials

Instead, I generally do a time & materials estimate - a fixed hourly rate applied to a good faith estimate of the range of time required to complete each component of the website. With this approach, the overall cost of the project is ultimately within your control.
